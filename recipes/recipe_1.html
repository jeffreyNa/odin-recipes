Strawberry-Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 Cup of Strawberries
  • 1/2 Cup of Frozen Blueberries
  • 1/4 Cup of Spinach
  • 1 teaspoon of Cinnamon
  • 1/2 Cup of Almond Milk
  • 1/2 Cup of Ice

Directions

  1. Put all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend all ingredients for 30 seconds
  3. Enjoy!
